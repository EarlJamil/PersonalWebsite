Hobbies

     Growing up, my passion for arts and crafts was deep. When I was a child, my days were filled with a variety of interests that offered me great joy. Painting was a favorite pastime of mine, as it allowed me to scatter colors on a canvas and let my imagination run wild. Each brushstroke was a means of expressing my emotions and creativity. Calligraphy was another pastime that attracted me. The skill of writing beautifully was both difficult and gratifying. I admired the pen's flowing movements and how each stroke created lovely letters. I remembered that I always use my savings just to buy some pens and tools for it. It was wild back then, I must say. Drawing was another activity that I really enjoyed also. A pencil in hand, I could draw things that I have in mind. I would say that teaching myself how to draw is not easy because it requires a lot of hand strength but I managed to push through it. Moving on to another form of art which is photography. Whenever I see something on the street or anywhere, I always capture it. Each photo has a story behind it. Back then, I didn't understand why people always seemed to capture the things or places they saw, but now, I realize it's the art of noticing. Noticing things, places, and experiences holds a huge amount of value. Photography is now my favorite hobby. It allows me to see the world through a different lens, appreciating the beauty in everyday moments. Through photography, I have learned to pay attention to the little details that make life extraordinary, and it has deepened my appreciation for the world around me. I just really adore how a device could hold a memory that could be preserved forever. All I can say is these hobbies that I have will forever be my hobbies in life.
